When I first learned about Doglas Trading, what struck me was their commitment to innovation and efficiency. What excites me most is the chance to apply everything I've learned—from enterprise systems to GenAI integration—in a fresh context. Every company has unique challenges, and I'm eager to dive in and understand how technology can drive real value here.

Starting a new job is always a mix of excitement and nerves. There's so much to learn—new systems, new people, new ways of working. But that's exactly what keeps this career path so rewarding. I'm ready to roll up my sleeves, contribute to the team, and help build something great.

To the team at Doglas Trading—thank you for this opportunity. I'm looking forward to the journey ahead.
